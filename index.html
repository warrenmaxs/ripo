Welcome to the Haven of Peace Lodge, No. 4385, Dar es Salaam, the home of Freemasonry in Tanzania, One of the oldest secular
social and charitable organisations in the world, modern Freemasonry’s roots lie in the traditions of the medieval stonemasons who
built our castles and cathedrals. For Freemasons, there are four important values that help define their path through life: Integrity, Friendship, Respect
and Service. In today’s world filled with uncertainty, these principles ring as true now as they have at any point in the
organisation’s history. Membership is open to men over 18 from all backgrounds and the organisation’s aim is to empower
members to be the best they can be – it’s about building character, supporting members as individuals and helping them make
a positive contribution to society. Freemasonry provides a structure for members to come together under these common goals,
enabling people to make new friendships, develop themselves and make valuable contributions to charitable causes. We are one
of the largest charitable givers in the country, contributing £51.1m to deserving causes in 2020.

Our Values

Maadili Yetu

UADILIFU

Badara ya kufanya kazi kwenye majengo ya kama waashi wa zamani, Freemason wa leo wanzingatia
kujijenga kama watu wa Uadilifu, na uanachama hutoa mundo/namna ya kusahidia kufikia lengo
hilo.

INTEGRITY

Rather than working on buildings like the masons of old, today’s Freemasons focus on
building themselves as people of integrity, and membership provides the structure to help
achieve that goal.

URAFIKI

Moja ya mashirika kongwe ya kijamii duniani, Freemasonry haijafafanuliwa na itikadi yoyote.
Ni wazi kwa watu kutoka dini zote, kabila na ushawishi wa kisiasa na hutoa msingi wa kwaida
wa urafiki kati ya wanachama, ambaowengi eao utadumu kwa maisha.

FRIENDSHIP

One of the oldest social organisations in the world, Freemasonry is not defined by an
ideology. It is open to people from all religions and political persuasions and provides
the common foundation for friendships between members, many of which will last for life.

HESHIMA

Pamoja na uanachama wa zaidi ya watu 16000 kutoka jumuiya kote Tanzania, Freemasonry huleta
watu pamojja bila kujali rangi zao, dini wala kabila au tofauti zozote zinazofikiriwa ambayo
inaweza kututenganisha kama jamii.

RESPECT

With a membership of more than 150,000 people drawn from communities across the UK, Freemasonry
brings people together irrespective of their race,religion or any other perceived differences
that can divide us as a society.

HUDUMA

Iwe ni kwa shirika katika makutano, kuchangisha fedha kwa ajili ya usaidizi au kujitolea kwa umma
au mashirika ya kijamii, huduma ni kiini cha Freemasonry. Wanachama wetu hufanya thamani michango
kwa kuchangia muda, rasilimali na ujenzi.

SERVICE

Whether participating in events, fundraising for a charitable cause or volunteering for public
or community organisations, service is at the very heart of Freemasonry. Our members make valuable
contributions by donating time, resources and skills.

HISTORIA NA MAFANIKIO YETU

Takriban muongo mmoja baada ya kuanzishwa kwa Grand Lodge ya Uingereza mnamo 1717, Freemasonry ilikuwa
kubwa zaidi na yenye ushawishi mkubwa zaidi kati ya vilabu na jamii nyingi za Uingereza. Hii iliendelea
hadi karne ya 20. Jambo kuu lilikuwa utangazaji ulioenea wa 1723 Katiba za
Freemasons, ambao walianzisha kanuni za kisasa za Kutaalamika:

  • Uvumilivu wa kidini, kitu chenye msimamo mkali kabisa katika ulimwengu wenye mizozo ya kidini;
  • Meritocracy, wakati kuzaliwa na mali kuliamua mafanikio;
  • Viwango vya juu vya ustaarabu baina ya watu;
  • Elimu ya kisayansi na kisanii;
  • Uboreshaji wa kibinafsi wa kijamii na kibinafsi.

JE, KUNA GHARAMA ZA KUJIUNGA?

Ndiyo. Kila loji huweka ada zake kwa wanachama wapya, kwa ujumla kati ya $100 na $300. wanachama wapya hulipa ada kusaidia shughuli za lodge.

Ada ya Kuanzisha: Lodge inatoza ada ya kuanzishwa kwa kila moja ya digrii tatu za Uashi: Mwanafunzi, Ufundi Wenzake, na Mwalimu Mason. Kwa kanuni,ada hii lazima ilipwe kabla a shahada inaweza kutolewa. Kila loji huweka ada kwa digrii zake, ambazo kwa kawaida hutegemea kati ya $100 na $300. Swali moja ambalo mwanamume anaweza kuulizwa anapotaka kujiunga ni. kama yeye anaweza kumudu ada hizi bila kujeruhi au kuinyima familia yake. Ikiwa kulipa ada itakuwa ngumu, anahimizwa kusubiri hadi hali yake ya kifedha itengenezwe.

Malipo ya Kila Mwaka ya Lodge: Kila Mwalimu Mwashi lazima alipe ada za kila mwaka ili kudumisha uanachama wake msimamo mzuri. Kiasi hiki hulipwa kila mwaka kabla ya mkutano wa mwaka wa nyumba ya kulala wageni na uchaguzi wa maafisa. Malipo ya kawaida ya nyumba ya kulala wageni ni takriban $100 kwa mwaka,kulingana na shughuli na eneo la nyumba ya kulala wageni. Nyumba za kulala wageni zilizo na programu amilifu za kijamii na familia au na majengo ya kusaidia inaweza kukusanya zaidi katika ada za kila mwaka.


Mara kwa mara, mwanachama atapata shida kulipa ada yake kwa sababu ya kupoteza kazi, gharama za matibabu, au vikwazo vingine vya kifedha. Wakati haya yanatokea,nyumba ya kulala wageni itafanya kazi na mwanachama kukidhi hali yake binafsi. Lengo la nyumba ya kulala wageni ni kuweka kila Mwalimu Mason mwanachama hai.
